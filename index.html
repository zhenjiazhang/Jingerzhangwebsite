About

Jinger Zhang is a multimedia bilingual business journalist from China based in New York. She is currently reporting on cryptocurrency and financial markets, striving to deliver complex financial information to a global audience in clear, accessible language.

She also has experience filming, editing, and directing documentaries. She tells social and economic stories through video, focusing on how immigration and economic policies impact working-class communities.

She is president of the Newmark School’s Asian American Journalists Association chapter and received the 2025 Deadline Club Scholarship from the New York chapter of the Society of Professional Journalists.
